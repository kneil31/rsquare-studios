ICE: "That was incredible — how are you feeling right now?"
Don't use in edit. Just let them relax.

INTERCUT: Dad's hand on her shoulder tonight (120p). Her walking confidently through party.

INTERCUT: Her being goofy at party. Parent's knowing eyes.

Rules

1. B-CAM never leaves her face (entrance, speeches, cake). Check every 15 min.
2. REACTION > ACTION. Dad's face during entrance > the entrance itself.
3. 120p BEFORE the moment. Entrance, candle blow, dance hug. Late = missed.
4. PARENTS' FACES = THE FILM. She is the subject. They are the story.
5. Shoot THROUGH the crowd. Foreground blur = cinematic.
6. THE INTERVIEW = THE FILM. "When did you realize she wasn't a little girl anymore?"
7. EMBRACE THE DARK. Dance floor DJ lights + high ISO = music video. Don't fight it.
8. SHE'S STILL A KID. Get the glam. Also get her being goofy. Contrast = truth.

Audio Settings

R6 II: Manual | Level 4 | Wind OFF | Noise OFF | Compression OFF | Wi-Fi OFF
DJI Mini: Stereo (S) | RX gain +9 dB | TX gain 0 | NC OFF
Rode GO II: Internal recording ON near birthday girl
Target: Peaks at -12 dB on R6 II meter
Dance floor: Lock manual Kelvin BEFORE party. Don't white balance for DJ lights.
