Birthday Party

Before Guests

Get these while the venue is empty
Empty venue push-in through door — film opener
Decor details — banner, balloons, table setup (shallow DOF)
Cake detail — unlit candle, frosting texture, topper
His outfit laid out — tiny shoes, birthday clothes
Record 30s room tone — mics rolling, no people audio

He Arrives + Party

Reactions over action — shoot the faces watching him
Mayank's entrance — walking in or being carried
His face reacting to the decorations / crowd
Parents watching him — stolen, unaware (long lens)
Grandparents holding him — tight on their face
Kids playing together — ground level 120p
Shoot THROUGH props / decor — foreground blur
His hands grabbing something — parent's finger, toy 120p
His laugh — get low, eye level 120p

Cake + Candle

Switch to 120p BEFORE candles are lit!
His face seeing the cake 120p
Room singing Happy Birthday audio
Candle blow — parent helping 120p
His reaction right after the blow
Cake smash / first touch — hands in frosting 120p

Wind Down

Tired on parent's shoulder — the crash
The mess — crumbs, wrapping paper, deflating balloons
Family together — pull back slow — FILM CLOSER

Interview — Parents

Quiet corner. Mic on collar. 5-7 min. Let them breathe.
Q1: "Talk to me about the night he came home from the hospital."
INTERCUT: Dad's hands holding him today (120p). Carrying through doorway.
Got Q1 answer
Q2: "What does his laugh feel like?"
INTERCUT: Mayank laughing (120p). Parent's smile at party.
Got Q2 answer
Q3: "One thing he does now that he'll stop doing soon?"
INTERCUT: The specific thing they describe. Grabbing finger (120p).
Got Q3 answer
Q4: "A 3 AM moment nobody else knows about?"
INTERCUT: Parent's tired eyes. Him doing something mundane.
Got Q4 answer
Q5: "He'll watch this at 18. What do you need him to hear?"
CLOSER. Let it breathe. If they cry — keep rolling. 5 sec silence after.
Got Q5 answer (THE closer)

Interview — Guests

Quick 30-sec each. Keep it fun.
"One piece of advice for Mayank?" (2-3 guests)
"What will he be when he grows up?" (2-3 guests)
"One thing about him that makes you smile?" (2-3 guests)
