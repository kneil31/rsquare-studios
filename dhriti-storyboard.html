Film Structure (Edit Guide)

Act 1: The Space (0:00-0:15)
Empty venue details. Cradle. Flowers. Name card. Music starts soft. No people yet.
Act 2: The Gathering (0:15-0:30)
Family arrives. Greetings. Baby being dressed. Dad watching. Energy building.
Act 3: The Ritual (0:30-1:30)
Core ceremony: pooja, gold ring, naming whisper, akshatalu. Intercut parent interview Q1-Q4 as voiceover. 120p hero moments. This is the heart — take your time.
Act 4: The Cradle (1:30-2:15)
Baby placed in cradle. Jola pata singing (use actual audio). Family takes turns. Aarti. Intercut Q5 (2 AM moment). Emotional peak.
Act 5: The Message (2:15-3:00)
Quick celebration montage. Then Q6 direct to camera: "Tell her something she needs to hear." Dhriti sleeping. Empty cradle with flowers. Fade.
Target: 2.5-3 min film. Interview audio as voiceover throughout — never on-screen talking heads. Music + mantras + jola pata singing = the real soundtrack.

Gear Quick Ref

C50: Wide DR | 4K 60p default | 120p S&F for hero moments
Lens: 15-35mm on gimbal (80%). 70-200mm handheld for tight reactions (20%)
Audio: DJI Mic Mini TX1 on pandit (mantras), TX2 on parent (interview). Rode GO on camera.
ISO: 800 if well-lit venue. 3200-6400 if dim. Dual base = no fear at 6400.
R5C: Photo cam. Parallel stills throughout. Focus on group shots + key ritual moments.
