Rules

1. B-CAM never leaves Anvika's face. Check every 15 min.
2. REACTION > ACTION. Shoot the faces watching.
3. 120p BEFORE the moment. Late = missed.
4. GET LOW. Her eye level = 60% of shots.
5. Shoot THROUGH props. Foreground blur = cinematic.
6. Don't over-direct. Let Anvika be Anvika.
7. Silence during interview pauses. Wait 5 seconds.
8. Not every shot needs movement. Static creates contrast.
