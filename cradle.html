Interview — Parents

Quiet corner. TX1 on collar. C-cam rolling. B-cam on hands. 5-7 min.
Swap DJI TX1 to parent collar (6-8" below chin, windscreen on)
Position B-cam on their hands from the side
ICE: "This was beautiful — how are you feeling right now?"
Don't use in edit. Just let them relax.
Q1: "What went through your mind the moment they placed him in the cradle?"
INTERCUT: baby being placed, parent's hands gripping each other.

INTERCUT: baby cooing/crying during ceremony, parent's smile.

Got Q6 answer (THE closer)

Rules

1. B-CAM never leaves baby's face during cradle placement. Check every 15 min.
2. REACTION > ACTION. Shoot the faces watching, not just the ritual.
3. 120p BEFORE the moment. Late = missed.
4. Cradle placement + name whisper = NON-NEGOTIABLE moments. Do NOT miss.
5. Shoot THROUGH pooja items — foreground blur = cinematic.
6. Audio on during lullabies + name whisper. Capture the songs.
7. Silence during interview pauses. Wait 5 seconds.
8. Not every shot needs movement. Static creates contrast.

Audio Settings

R6 II: Manual | Level 4 | Wind OFF | Noise OFF | Compression OFF | Wi-Fi OFF
DJI Mini: Stereo (S) | RX gain +9 dB | TX gain 0 | NC OFF
Rode GO II: Internal recording ON near baby
Target: Peaks at -12 dB on R6 II meter
